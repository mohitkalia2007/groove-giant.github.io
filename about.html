What is Groove Giant?

Groove Giant is a desktop app designed to assist beginners in transcribing their own jazz solos.

FAQ

How can I use it?

In the desktop app, select a file and adjust parameters like tempo and key accordingly. Then, Groove Giant will transcribe your musical audio and display sheet music with musical analysis.

What's the analysis?

Groove Giant will identify chord tones, available tensions, and enclosures based on the given chord changes.

How does it work?

Groove Giant uses Short-time Fourier Transforms to decode musical audio into pitches, and converts those pitches into a sheet music stream. Then, it uses algorithms to analyze the transcription.

About the developer

Hey! My name is Mohit Kalia, a senior at Bergen County Academies High School in New Jersey. As a saxophonist, I struggled a lot with transcribing solos when starting out in jazz, particularly my own. Transcribing your own solos is incredibly valuable in analyzing and improving your own improvisation, but its also time-consuming and difficult, especially as a beginner. Thus, I developed Groove Giant, an app that solves that issue and encourages beginners to keep transcribing.
