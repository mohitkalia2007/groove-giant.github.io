MuseScore 4 MUST be installed for this to run.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of files can I use?

File types include mp3, wav, and flac.

Can I use files with accompaniment?

Only monophonic files are supported (i.e. saxophone without accompaniment)

Why are triplets/sixteenth notes not shown?

The current version of Groove Giant only transcribers quarter and eighth notes.

Why is the produced sheet music in a different key than my audio file?

The transcription will be in concert key, so Bb and Eb instruments will not receive a transcription in their key.

Isnt transcription supposed to be helpful?

Transcribing masters in jazz is arguably the most important exercise to improve improvisation. Transcribing your own solos provides lots of opportunities for growth, but it takes away lots of time which couldve been used to transcribe masters.
